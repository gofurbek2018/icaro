What is Icaro?

Icaro is complete Hotspot written in Go and Vue.js. It uses CoovaChilli as access controller which can be configured and installed inside NethServer.

Free WiFi access

Give your business customers Internet access with social login or create paid vauchers.

Fully Open Source

Icaro is fully Open Source, free of charge and distributed under AGPL license.

Deploy in minutes

Deploy Icaro in minutes using Vagrant and Ansible.
